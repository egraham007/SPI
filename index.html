Methodology

How SIR is calculated

A single transparent formula built around how swimmers actually contribute at conference championships.

STEP 01
Percentile mapping

Each time is mapped against the conference's percentile distribution for that event and season. Where you finish in the distribution — not the raw time — is what matters.

STEP 02
SIR score

Scores run from 1.000 (best) upward — like golf. Lower is better. A 1.000 is the top of the conference. A 3.000 is average. A 5.000 is at the bottom of the field.

STEP 03
Context shift

The same SIR process runs independently for each conference using that conference's benchmarks. A swimmer ranked #42 in the ACC might rank #3 in the Patriot League — that's the point.
